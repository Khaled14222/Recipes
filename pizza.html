Easy Homemade Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 1 package active dry yeast (0.25 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Activate the Yeast:
    • In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar.
    • Let stand for 10 minutes, or until foamy.
  2. Mix the Dough:
    • Add flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture.
    • Knead the dough until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer with a dough hook.
  3. First Rise:
    • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough and shape it into a ball.
    • Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired shape and thickness.
  5. Prepare the Pizza:
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
    • Top with your favorite pizza sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  6. Bake the Pizza:
    • Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
    • Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
