Moussaka

Ingredients

  • 3 eggplants, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick slices
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon fines herbs
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ cup red wine

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Eggplant:
    • Lay eggplant slices on paper towels; sprinkle lightly with salt.
    • Let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture, then pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Fry the Eggplant:
    • Warm olive oil in a skillet over high heat.
    • Fry eggplant until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
    • Drain on paper towels; set aside.
  3. Cook the Meat Sauce:
    • Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Stir in ground beef, onions, and garlic; season with salt and black pepper.
    • Cook and stir until beef is browned, 8 to 10 minutes.
    • Add parsley, fines herbs, cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg.
    • Pour in tomato sauce and wine; mix well.
    • Simmer for 20 minutes.
    • Allow to cool, then stir in beaten egg.
